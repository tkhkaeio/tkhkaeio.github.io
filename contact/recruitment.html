Working with Me

I'm open to research collaborations and internship mentoring for highly motivated individuals who are excited about computer vision, machine learning, and robotics.

For students interested in the Research Intern (VLAs) role at AIRoA, please apply through the official openings on the Careers page. The internship provides the opportunity to work with an international and cross-functional team on cutting-edge VLA research.

Over the past years, students and collaborators I mentored have published their research findings at major conferences such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ICLR, WACV. Many of these projects grew from small exploratory ideas into full research contributions. If you enjoy building things from scratch, you will likely fit well! Feel free to contact me!
